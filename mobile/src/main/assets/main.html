Thank you for using TawaafOnWear



This app was developed to be as simple as possible lest it become a distraction to your worship.

Therefore, please NOT use this application if it, or your Wear device does become a distraction.
Preferably, turn off your phone or Bluetooth, so that other notifications stop. Start your tawaaf by making a clear, sincere intention. Recite the takbeer at the Hajre-Aswad (Black stone) and proceed to do your 1st round, constantly in connection with your Creator. When you reach the Hajre-aswad again, tap your phone to record the round. Repeat until the end of the 7th round, after which the counter will reset and the tawaaf count will increment.

Please remember me in your duahs.
